Overview

I am striving to secure a global remote job for two primary reasons:

  • Moving to India to stay close to my parents.
  • Having the freedom to move around.

The process of landing a global remote job differs significantly from securing a typical FAANG role. While there are abundant resources for FAANG job preparation, the key aspects typically include:

  • Proficiency in algorithms, i.e., solving two medium-level Leetcode questions within an hour.
  • For senior roles, a strong understanding of System Design concepts is essential.
